# Product

## Register

product

## Users

HR Admin and HR Manager are the primary daily users — they manage recruitment pipelines, employee records, and access control all day. Unit Heads and Directors check in occasionally to review candidates or approve decisions. Employees access the system infrequently, mainly for personal profile tasks. All users are hospital staff, not tech-savvy power users, working in a professional healthcare environment.

## Product Purpose

ATS RS Azra is an internal Applicant Tracking System and employee management tool for Rumah Sakit Azra. It centralizes recruitment workflows, role-based access, and HR data into a single platform. Success means HR staff can move candidates through pipelines and manage employee data without friction — fewer clicks, no confusion, reliable data.

## Brand Personality

Modern, clean, approachable. A system that feels trustworthy and calm, not cold or bureaucratic. Warm enough for daily use, efficient enough for power users.

## Anti-references

- Old government portals: no dense tables, no ugly form layouts, no 2005 visual aesthetic
- Avoid the generic SaaS template look (off-white + rounded cards + teal accent repeated everywhere)

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over density** — HR users scan many records at a time; visual hierarchy must be obvious without noise
2. **Warm efficiency** — approachable enough for daily use, fast enough to not slow down power users
3. **Structure without stiffness** — organized like Notion: calm, spacious, content-forward, not bureaucratic
4. **Trust through consistency** — a system people rely on must look and behave predictably across every screen
5. **Progressive disclosure** — show only what is needed now; surfaces details on demand without overwhelming

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Sufficient color contrast for all text and interactive elements. No reliance on color alone to convey meaning.
